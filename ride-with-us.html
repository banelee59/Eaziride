Ride with Us

Knowing Who Is Behind the Wheels

The use of profile pictures to identify drivers does not work with us. Your safety is our priority, and we ensure our driver-owners undergo thorough background checks before using the platform.

Ride with Peace of Mind

We provide riders with real-time status information about vehicle condition, passenger liability insurance, driver-owner criminal record, and much more. We want to ensure you get to your destination safely without a doubt.

More than a Ride

Your trust in our ride-sharing platform opens exciting products, specifically affordable data and voice packages so you can stay connected with friends and family while enjoying a riding experience.

Safety for Everyone

We have introduced a great feature for riders to seamlessly communicate with their families, friends, and, where needed, with driver-owners during the ride. Choosing your favorite driver-owner, male or female, when requesting a ride has never been so super easy.
